# Chapter 1: Introduction to Robotics

## Learning Objectives

After completing this chapter, you will be able to:
- Define robotics and its applications
- Identify the main components of a robotic system
- Understand the history and evolution of robotics
- Recognize different types of robots and their classifications
- Explain the fundamental principles of robot control

## Overview

This chapter provides an introduction to the fascinating world of robotics. We'll explore what robots are, how they work, and their applications in various industries. From industrial automation to service robots, we'll cover the breadth of modern robotics.
