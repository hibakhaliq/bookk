# Chapter 1: Purpose and Focus

## Purpose

The purpose of this chapter is to provide students with a foundational understanding of robotics, its history, and its applications. Students will learn about the fundamental components of robotic systems and the principles that govern their operation.

## Focus Areas

1. **Historical Context**: Understanding the evolution of robotics from ancient automata to modern AI-driven systems
2. **Robot Components**: Identifying and understanding the key components of robotic systems (sensors, actuators, controllers)
3. **Robot Classifications**: Learning different ways to categorize robots (by application, mobility, control method)
4. **Applications**: Exploring real-world applications of robotics across various industries
5. **Ethics and Safety**: Introducing ethical considerations and safety protocols in robotics

## Learning Outcomes

By the end of this chapter, students will:
- Understand the basic definition of a robot and distinguish it from other automated systems
- Recognize the main components of a robotic system and their functions
- Classify different types of robots based on various criteria
- Identify key applications of robotics in industry and daily life
- Appreciate the historical development of robotics and its future potential

## Prerequisites

Students should have:
- Basic understanding of mechanical systems
- Elementary knowledge of electronics
- Familiarity with programming concepts (helpful but not required)

## Technical Depth Level

**Beginner** - This chapter is designed for students with minimal background in robotics. Technical concepts are explained using accessible language with practical examples to illustrate key points.
